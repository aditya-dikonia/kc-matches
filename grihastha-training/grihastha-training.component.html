Grihastha Training

WE CAN HELP YOU MAKE A BETTER CHOICE

Kcmatches partners with you from the very beginning. At the very outset of your decision to start looking for a prospective groom/ bride, kcmatches helps you to develop a holistic perspective towards marriage. Through grihastha training we provide assistance in the following ways:

  • A personalised guidance into how a life partner can be chosen
  • Setting expectations and assessing the future trajectory of life
  • How to turn to Prabhupada's teachings for a successful married life
  • How to protect oneself from common pitfalls
  • Counselling by senior, experienced grihasthas
